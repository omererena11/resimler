OE Apps Studio Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to mobile applications and digital products developed by OE Apps Studio. It summarizes how data of users of our applications is processed, which services may be used, and how user information is protected.

Scope

This policy covers the general privacy framework for mobile applications, games, and connected digital services published by OE Apps Studio. Some applications may have different technical features; however, our data processing approach is essentially simple, limited, and prioritizes user security.

Information collected

Our applications generally do not aim to collect personal data directly from the user. Most applications do not request name, address, ID information or similar personal data.

Some applications may process limited technical data through third-party services to ensure technical operation or to serve ads.

Purpose of data use

Data sharing

OE Apps Studio does not sell, rent, or share user data directly with third parties for commercial purposes. However, advertising, notification, or analytics infrastructures used in applications may process limited data as a technical necessity.

Children's privacy

Our applications are not intentionally designed to collect personal data from children. If a parent or legal guardian believes that inappropriate data of a child has been processed, they can contact us.

Data security

We value user security. We take care not to collect unnecessary data in our applications and choose the services used as limited and appropriate as possible. However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Policy updates

This privacy policy may be updated when necessary. The updated version becomes effective as soon as it is published on this page. Continuing to use our applications means you accept the current policy.
